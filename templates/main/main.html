About

The Online Financial Bank Management System is a project that aims to create an online banking platform using Python and its libraries. The system allows customers to securely access their accounts and perform various financial transactions, such as depositing, withdrawing, transferring, or checking their balance. It also helps the user to analyze their expenses by showing a pie chart and a bar graph of the expenditure according to the categories where the money has been spent. The system is designed to be user-friendly, comprehensive, and robust, ensuring a smooth and convenient banking experience.
